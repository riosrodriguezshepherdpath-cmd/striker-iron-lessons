Week 4 Day 4: Math - Percent Change

Coach's Script: What's up striker! To improve, you need to track your progress! If you did 10 juggling tricks last week and 12 today, you improved! By how much? That's what we are learning today: Percentage of Change! Let's measure our growth!

Phase 1: The Percent Change Formula

To find the percent change, follow these steps:

1. Find the difference: Subtract the old number from the new number.

2. Divide: Divide the difference by the original number.

3. Convert: Multiply by 100 to make it a percentage.

üí° SIMPLE IDEA: (Change / Original) x 100 = % Change.
