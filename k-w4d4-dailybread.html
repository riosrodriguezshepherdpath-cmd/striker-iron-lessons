Week 4 Day 4: Daily Bread - Contentment

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Yesterday we talked about endurance. Today is about contentment and gratitude! It is easy to complain when you don't get your way or when you want better gear. But a true striker is thankful for the opportunity to play and grateful for the team they have. Let's focus on what we have!

Phase 1: What is Contentment?

Contentment is being satisfied with what you have right now.

Gratitude is being thankful for those things.

A true striker doesn't complain about the field conditions or the weather; they just play!

Phase 3: Practical Application

How do we use this? When you want a new pair of cleats but can't get them, do you complain?

Gratitude means taking care of the cleats you have and saying thanks!

Contentment means being happy with your role on the team, even if you want to be a striker!
