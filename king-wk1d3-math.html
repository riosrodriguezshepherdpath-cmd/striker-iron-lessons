Week 1 Day 3: Basic Fractions

"What's up, striker! As our center midfielder (*el mediocampista*), you divide the field between defense and attack. Today, we're dividing numbers too!"
PHASE 1: What is a Fraction?

A fraction is a part of a whole. Imagine one whole pizza divided for the team.

PHASE 2: Numerator and Denominator

The top number is the numerator. It is how many parts we have. The bottom is the denominator. It is the total parts.

üîë KEY POINT: Denominator = Down. It's on the bottom!
PHASE 3: Soccer Fractions

If you take 4 shots and score on 1, your fraction is 1/4. You scored one out of four!

‚öΩ SOCCER TIP: 1/2 of the game is defense, 1/2 is offense!
PHASE 4: Practice Problems

1. If 10 players are on the field, and 3 are defenders, what fraction are defenders?

2. If a game has 4 quarters, what fraction is 1 quarter?

PHASE 5: Physical Mission

Find 3 items in your house that can be divided (like a carton of eggs, a pizza, or a book with chapters). Write down the fraction for one part of that item.
