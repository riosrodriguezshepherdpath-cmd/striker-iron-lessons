Week 4 Day 1: Daily Bread - Focus

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Today we are talking about focus and discipline. That means keeping your eyes on the ball even when the crowd is loud! A true striker doesn't let distractions stop them from making the shot. Let's keep our minds sharp!

Phase 1: What is Focus?

Focus is giving your full attention to one thing.

On the field, it means watching the ball, not the defenders' feet.

Discipline is doing what needs to be done, even when you don't feel like it.

The Bible says, "Let your eyes look straight ahead; fix your gaze directly before you" (Proverbs 4:25).

This means we don't look back at mistakes, and we don't look away from our goals.

God wants us to have a disciplined heart.

Phase 3: Practical Application

How do we use this? When you miss a shot, do you get angry and stop focusing?

Discipline means shaking it off and focusing on the next play immediately.

It means turning off the phone during study time!

  • I will shake off mistakes immediately.
  • I will turn off distractions.
