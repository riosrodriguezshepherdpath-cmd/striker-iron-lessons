Rocket Propulsion
‚è± 7 MINUTES
Action Force: Rocket engine pushes hot gas *down/back*. Reaction Force: Hot gas pushes rocket *up/forward*.
Rockets operate in a vacuum because they do not require atmospheric air to create thrust.
