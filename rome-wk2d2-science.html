F=ma Concept
‚è± 6 MINUTES
Sir, F=ma means Force (F) equals Mass (m) times Acceleration (a). If you apply more force, you get more acceleration. If the mass is heavier, you need more force for the same acceleration.
Acceleration is directly proportional to force and inversely proportional to mass.
