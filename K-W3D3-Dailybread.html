Coach's Script: What's up striker! Today we are talking about coachability. That means listening to instruction and trying to apply it right away! A true striker wants to learn and improve every single day. Let's learn to listen better!

Coachability is being willing to learn from others.

On the field, it means listening to the coach without complaining.

It means making the change the coach told you to make.
