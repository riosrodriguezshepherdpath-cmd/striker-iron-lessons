What Is A Fraction?

A fraction shows PART of something.

It has two numbers.
üîë KEY POINT: Fractions have a TOP number and a BOTTOM number

The Top Number (Numerator)
‚è±Ô∏è 5 minutes
The TOP number is called the NUMERATOR.
Big word! Let's just call it the "top number."
5/10
The 5 is the TOP number.
It tells you HOW MANY parts you have.
üîë KEY POINT: Top number = How many parts you have
PIZZA EXAMPLE: A pizza has 8 slices. You eat 3 slices. The top number is 3. That's how many YOU have.
SOCCER EXAMPLE: Your team plays 10 games. You win 7 games. The top number is 7. That's how many you WON.
The top number is always smaller than or equal to the bottom number.
You can't eat 10 slices if the pizza only has 8 slices!

The Bottom Number (Denominator)
‚è±Ô∏è 5 minutes
The BOTTOM number is called the DENOMINATOR.
Let's just call it the "bottom number."
5/10
The 10 is the BOTTOM number.
It tells you how many TOTAL parts there are.
üîë KEY POINT: Bottom number = Total parts
PIZZA EXAMPLE: The pizza has 8 total slices. The bottom number is 8. That's the WHOLE pizza.
SOCCER EXAMPLE: Your team plays 10 total games. The bottom number is 10. That's the WHOLE season.
The bottom number tells you how the whole thing is divided.
If the bottom number is 4, the whole is cut into 4 equal parts.
