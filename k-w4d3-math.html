Week 4 Day 3: Math - Conversions

Coach's Script: What's up striker! To be a smart player, you need to know that 50%, 0.5, and 1/2 are all the same thing! Sometimes it's easier to use a fraction, sometimes a decimal. Today we learn how to switch between them instantly! Let's go!

Phase 1: Decimal to Percent

To turn a decimal into a percentage, move the decimal point two places to the **right** and add the % sign.

Example: 0.85 = 85%

Example: 0.05 = 5%
