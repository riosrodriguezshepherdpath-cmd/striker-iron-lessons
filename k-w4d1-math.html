Week 4 Day 1: Math - Understanding Percentages

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Today we are learning about percentages. You see these on TV when they talk about shooting accuracy or how much time is left in a game! "Percent" means "out of 100". Let's master this skill!

Phase 1: What is a Percentage?

Percentage means "parts per hundred".

If you have 100% of something, you have all of it.

If you have 50%, you have half of it.

Phase 2: Percentages, Fractions, and Decimals

Percentages can be written as fractions or decimals.

25% = 25/100 = 1/4 = 0.25
50% = 50/100 = 1/2 = 0.50
75% = 75/100 = 3/4 = 0.75
  • I can write percentages as fractions.
  • I can write percentages as decimals.

Phase 3: Soccer Percentages

If you take 10 shots and make 8, your percentage is 80% (80 out of 100 if you took 100 shots).

To find the percentage: divide the number made by the total attempted, then multiply by 100.

  • I know how to calculate a simple percentage.
  • I understand shooting percentage.

Phase 4: Mental Check

What is 10% as a fraction?

Phase 5: Physical Mission - Percentage Passing

Take 10 passes at a target (like a cone or wall).

Calculate your passing percentage based on how many hit the target.

If you hit 7/10, your percentage is 70%!
