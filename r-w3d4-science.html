Action-Reaction Pairs
‚è± 7 MINUTES
Forces occur in equal, opposite pairs. They act on *different* objects, which is why they do not cancel out.
Action: Object A exerts a force on Object B. Reaction: Object B exerts an equal and opposite force on Object A.
