Week 2 Day 4: Daily Bread - Humility

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Today we are talking about humility. That means not acting like you are better than your teammates! A true striker helps the team win, even if someone else scores the goal. Let's work together!

Phase 1: What is Humility?

Humility is not thinking you are better than others.

On the field, it means passing the ball instead of dribbling alone.

It means listening to the coach and helping teammates improve.
