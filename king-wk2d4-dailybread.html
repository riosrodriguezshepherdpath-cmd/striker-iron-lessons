Week 2 Day 4: Daily Bread - Integrity

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Today we are talking about integrity. That means doing the right thing even when no one is watching! A true striker plays fair, even if the referee missed a foul. Let's build strong character!

Phase 1: What is Integrity?

Integrity is being honest and having strong moral principles.

On the field, it means not diving or cheating to get a call.

It means playing by the rules every single second of the game.

  • I know integrity means being honest.
  • I know to play fair on the field.
  • I understand that character matters.
‚òï QUICK BREAK: Stand up and stretch!

Phase 2: Biblical Foundation

The Bible says, "Whoever walks in integrity walks securely" (Proverbs 10:9).

When you have integrity, you don't have to worry about getting caught!

God cares about your character more than winning a game.

  • I know the verse Proverb 10:9.
  • I understand why integrity keeps me secure.
  • I'm ready to learn more.
‚òï HALFWAY THERE! Take a 1-minute break!

Phase 3: Practical Application

How do we use this? When you practice alone, do you skip reps?

Integrity means finishing all your drills, even if you are tired.

It means admitting if the ball went out off you.

  • I know not to skip practice reps.
  • I will admit if the ball went out.
  • I am building trust with my team.

Phase 4: Cursive Practice

Write neatly and take your time, striker.

Whoever walks in integrity walks securely.
Whoever walks in integrity walks securely.
Whoever walks in integrity walks securely.

Take a picture and submit with your worksheet!

  • I wrote the verse 3 times in cursive.
  • I wrote neatly.
  • I took a picture to submit.
‚òï ALMOST DONE! One more phase!

Phase 5: Physical Mission - Character Challenge

Perform one soccer drill today (like juggling) with perfect form.

If you make a mistake, start over!

This shows integrity in your training.
