Week 4 Day 1: Science - Potential and Kinetic Energy

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Today we are learning how to create power for your shots! We need to understand the difference between stored energy and moving energy. Let's get energetic!

Phase 1: Potential Energy (Stored)

Potential energy is stored energy based on an object's position or shape.

When you pull your leg back to kick, it's not moving yet, but it has potential energy ready to be used.

Phase 2: Kinetic Energy (Moving)

Kinetic energy is the energy of motion.

When you swing your leg forward and kick the ball, your leg and the ball have kinetic energy.

üí° SIMPLE IDEA: Kinetic = Moving energy (Energy of motion).
  • I know kinetic energy is moving energy.
  • I know speed increases kinetic energy.

Phase 3: Energy Transfer

In soccer, you transfer energy from your leg to the ball.

Your leg has kinetic energy, and it hits the ball, transferring that energy to make the ball fly!

  • I understand energy transfers when kicking.
  • I know kicking harder transfers more energy.

Phase 4: Mental Check

When a ball is sitting at the top of a hill, does it have potential or kinetic energy?

  • I know the answer is Potential.
  • I understand why.
