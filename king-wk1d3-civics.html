Week 1 Day 3: Local vs. State Government

"What's up, striker! Just like a team needs a coach *and* a league manager, our country has different levels of government!"
PHASE 1: Local Government

Local government is for your town or city. They manage things close to home.

üîë KEY POINT: The Mayor is like the team captain for the whole city.
PHASE 2: State Government

State government manages the whole state. They make laws that affect many cities.

‚öΩ SOCCER TIP: The State Governor is like the league commissioner for all teams.
PHASE 3: Comparing Roles

Local fixes your street; State builds the big highway connecting cities.

PHASE 4: Services Check

Local services: Police, Fire, Library, Public Schools, Trash. State services: State Police, Highways, Parks, Universities.

PHASE 5: Physical Mission

Go outside and find 3 things managed by local government (a stop sign, a library, a park). Take a picture of one!
