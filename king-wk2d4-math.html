Week 2 Day 4: Math - Penalty Kicks

Coach's Script: Listen up striker! Today is all about speed and accuracy. Different denominators are just defenders trying to stop you. We are going to find a common denominator, blow past them, and score! Let's work!

Phase 1: The Fast Breakdown

You cannot add different-sized pieces of the field!

Find the Least Common Denominator (LCD) to make them match.

Multiply to change the denominators so they are the same.

  • I know to find the LCD.
