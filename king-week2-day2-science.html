Week 2 Day 2: Newton's 3rd Law (Action/Reaction)

"What's up, striker! For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction! If you push the field, the field pushes you back!"
PHASE 1: Action and Reaction

Newton's 3rd Law says forces always come in pairs. If Object A pushes Object B, Object B pushes Object A back with the same force, but opposite direction.

üîë KEY POINT: Pushing = Being Pushed Back!
PHASE 2: Soccer Action

When you kick a soccer ball, your foot exerts a force on the ball (Action). The ball exerts an equal force back on your foot (Reaction)!

‚öΩ SOCCER TIP: The harder you kick, the harder the ball hits your foot back.
PHASE 3: Running Acceleration

To run forward, you push backward on the ground. The ground pushes you forward!

PHASE 4: F=ma + Pairs

You need Newton's 2nd law (Force) to accelerate, and 3rd law (Pairs) to create that force!

PHASE 5: Physical Mission

Stand near a wall. Push against the wall as hard as you can. Do you move? No, because the wall is pushing back! Now, jump off the ground‚Äîyou push the ground down, and the ground pushes you up!

üìñ BIBLE CONNECTION: "I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me." (Philippians 4:13). When we push forward, God provides the reaction strength!
