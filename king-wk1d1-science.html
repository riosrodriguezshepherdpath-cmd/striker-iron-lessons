PHASE 1: What is Inertia?

Inertia is the tendency of an object to resist a change in motion. Basically, objects want to keep doing what they are already doing.

üîë KEY POINT: An object at rest stays at rest unless acted on by an unbalanced force.
PHASE 2: Unbalanced Forces

To change an object's motion, you need an unbalanced force. This is a push or a pull that isn't canceled out by another force.

‚öΩ SOCCER TIP: Your kick is the unbalanced force that gets the ball moving!
PHASE 3: Objects in Motion

Objects in motion stay in motion at the same speed and in the same direction unless acted on by an unbalanced force.

üîë KEY POINT: Friction and gravity are forces that stop moving objects on Earth.
