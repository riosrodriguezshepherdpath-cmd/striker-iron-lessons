Phase 2: Action and Reaction in Soccer

Phase 3: Running and Jumping

To run, you push your foot backward against the ground.

The ground pushes your foot forward (Reaction), which moves your body forward!

To jump high, push down hard on the ground!

  • I know running is pushing the ground back.
  • I know jumping is pushing the ground down.

Phase 4: Mental Check
