Week 3 Day 2: Math - Multiplying Fractions

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Today we are learning a new trick: multiplying fractions! This is actually faster than adding because you don't need a common denominator! You just multiply straight across. Let's make some goals!

Phase 1: The Secret Formula

Multiply the tops (numerators) together.

Multiply the bottoms (denominators) together.

That's it! No need to find common denominators.
