Week 3 Day 2: Science - Acceleration

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Yesterday we learned about speed and velocity. Today, we are learning about acceleration! This is how fast your velocity changes. When you sprint, stop, or turn, you are accelerating! Let's get ready to accelerate!

Phase 1: What is Acceleration?

Acceleration is the rate at which velocity changes.

It means your speed is changing, your direction is changing, or both!

Phase 2: Three Ways to Accelerate

1. Speed Up: Going from walking to sprinting.

2. Slow Down (Decelerate): Stopping suddenly for the ball.

3. Change Direction: Turning left while dribbling.

  • I know speeding up is acceleration.
  • I know turning is acceleration.

Phase 3: The Importance of Acceleration

Speed is good, but acceleration is better for soccer!

You need to accelerate to get away from defenders or change direction quickly.

  • I know acceleration helps me pass defenders.
  • I know it helps me stop quickly.

Phase 4: Mental Check

If you are jogging at the same speed, are you accelerating?

  • I know the answer is NO (if speed and direction are same).
  • I understand acceleration requires a CHANGE.
