Understanding Ratios
‚è± 6 MINUTES
A ratio compares two quantities. It can be written as a fraction, with a colon (:), or using the word "to". Ratios should always be simplified.
Ratios must be expressed in the same units whenever possible.

Simplifying Ratios
‚è± 6 MINUTES
