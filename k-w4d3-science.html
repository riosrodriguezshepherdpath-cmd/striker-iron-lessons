Phase 2: Newton‚Äôs Second Law ($F=ma$)

Force equals Mass times Acceleration ($F = m \times a$).

Force: How hard you kick.

Mass: How heavy the ball is.

Acceleration: How fast the ball speeds up.

Higher Mass = More Force needed for acceleration.
Higher Force = More Acceleration.
