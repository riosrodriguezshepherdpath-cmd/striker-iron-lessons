When They Disagree
‚è±Ô∏è 5 minutes
Sometimes local and state government don't agree on things.
When this happens, state government usually wins. Here's why:
üîë KEY POINT: State government has more power than local government
Think of it like this: The state is the league. Your city is one team in the league.
If a team's rule disagrees with a league rule, the league rule wins!

REAL EXAMPLE: Plastic Bag Ban

Some cities wanted to ban plastic bags at stores. But the state said "No, cities can't make that rule." The state won because state law is stronger than city law.

This makes sense! Otherwise, every city could have completely different rules. That would be confusing!
