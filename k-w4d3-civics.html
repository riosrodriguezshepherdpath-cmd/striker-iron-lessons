Week 4 Day 3: Civics - State Government

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Today we are looking at the middle level of government: The State! Think of the Federal government as the league directors, the State government as the club directors, and the Local government as the team coaches. They all work together to keep the game running! Let's learn about the State level!

Phase 1: What is State Government?

State government manages issues that affect the whole state, but aren't handled by the national government.

They are led by a Governor.

They make laws for things like driver's licenses, state highways, and public universities.

Phase 3: The Three Branches at State Level

Just like the federal government, state governments have three branches:

1. Legislative: Makes state laws.

2. Executive: Governor enforces state laws.

3. Judicial: State courts interpret state laws.

[Image of three branches of government diagram]
