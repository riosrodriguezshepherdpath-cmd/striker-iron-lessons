PROFESSIONAL RESPONSE PROTOCOLS

FORMAL STATUS INQUIRY RESPONSES
Estoy bien, gracias. ¿Y usted?
(eh-STOY bee-EN, GRAH-see-ahs. ee oo-STED)
I am well, thank you. And you?
Standard professional response with reciprocal inquiry
Muy bien, gracias
(moo-EE bee-EN, GRAH-see-ahs)
Very well, thank you
Enhanced positive response for superior interactions
COURTESY EXPRESSIONS
Por favor
(por fah-VOR)
Please
Mandatory in all request contexts
Muchas gracias
(MOO-chahs GRAH-see-ahs)
Thank you very much
Enhanced gratitude for significant assistance
De nada
(deh NAH-dah)
You're welcome
Standard response to gratitude
Con mucho gusto
(kon MOO-choh GOO-stoh)
With much pleasure
Elevated courtesy response
AFFIRMATIVE RESPONSES
Sí, por supuesto
(see, por soo-PWEH-stoh)
Yes, of course
Affirmative with emphasis for superior requests
Sí, con mucho gusto
(see, kon MOO-choh GOO-stoh)
Yes, with much pleasure
Enthusiastic affirmative showing service orientation
NEGATIVE RESPONSES (SOFTENED)
Lo siento, pero no es posible
(loh see-EN-toh, PEH-roh noh es poh-SEE-bleh)
I'm sorry, but it's not possible
Softened negative maintaining professional relationship
Never issue unqualified "No" in professional contexts. Always provide explanation or express regret.
ATTENTION & INTERRUPTION
Disculpe
(dees-KOOL-peh)
Excuse me (formal)
Attention-getting with usted-level contacts
Disculpe la interrupción
(dees-KOOL-peh lah een-teh-roop-see-OHN)
Excuse the interruption
Necessary interruption with explicit apology
¿Podría repetir, por favor?
(poh-DREE-ah reh-peh-TEER, por fah-VOR)
Could you repeat, please?
Clarification request maintaining formal register
STANDARD PROFESSIONAL EXCHANGE:
1. Buenos días
2. ¿Cómo está usted?
3. Estoy bien, gracias. ¿Y usted?
4. Muy bien, muchas gracias
5. [Business matter with por favor]
6. Muchas gracias por su tiempo
7. De nada. Que tenga un buen día
Always maintain formal register (usted) until explicitly invited to switch. Register consistency signals professional competence.
