Week 4 Day 3: Daily Bread - Endurance

Coach's Script: What's up striker! We've covered focus and integrity. Today is about endurance! Soccer games are long, and sometimes you're tired, down by a goal, or the other team is stronger. Perseverance means keeping your energy up and fighting until the final whistle! Let's build toughness!

Phase 1: What is Endurance?

Endurance is the ability to keep going even when you are physically tired.

Perseverance is the mental toughness to keep trying even when you fail.

A true striker runs hard in the 90th minute just like the 1st minute!
