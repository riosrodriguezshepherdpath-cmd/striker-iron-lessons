Week 4 Day 2: Math - Calculating Percentages

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Yesterday we defined percentages. Today, we calculate them! If a team has 100 players, and 20% are defenders, how many defenders are there? Easy, right? We just need to multiply the total by the percentage (in decimal form). Let's go!

Phase 1: The Three Steps

1. Convert the percentage to a decimal (move the decimal point two places to the left).

2. Multiply that decimal by the total number.

3. Solve for the answer.
