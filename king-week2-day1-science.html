PHASE 3: Mass Matters

Mass is how heavy something is. It is harder to move heavy things!

‚öΩ SOCCER TIP: A soccer ball has low mass, a medicine ball has high mass.

If you want to move a high-mass object fast, you need a huge force!

PHASE 5: Physical Mission
