Week 4 Day 2: Civics - Local Government

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Yesterday we talked about the national level. Today, we are bringing it home! Local government is about your city or town. Think of them like the team management that keeps the field safe and ready for us to play! Let's learn how they work!

Phase 1: What is Local Government?

Local government manages services for a specific city or county.

They handle things like schools, fire departments, libraries, and parks.

They are managed by a Mayor or City Manager.
