Week 3 Day 1: Rights and Responsibilities

"What's up, striker! Being a citizen is like being on a team. You get benefits (Rights), but you also have jobs to do (Responsibilities)!"
PHASE 1: Rights (The Benefits)

Rights are freedoms we are born with. In America, we have the right to speak freely and practice our faith.

üîë KEY POINT: Rights = Freedoms protected by law.
PHASE 2: Responsibilities (The Job)

Responsibilities are things we *should* do to be good citizens. Like voting or following the rules.

‚öΩ SOCCER TIP: You have a right to play, but a responsibility to train!
PHASE 3: Balance

Rights and responsibilities work together. To keep our freedom, we must be responsible.

PHASE 4: Citizenship

Good citizens respect the rights of others and fulfill their own duties.

PHASE 5: Physical Mission

Create a "Rights & Responsibilities" chart for your soccer team. List 2 rights (e.g., playtime) and 2 responsibilities (e.g., bringing water) and share it with your coach.
