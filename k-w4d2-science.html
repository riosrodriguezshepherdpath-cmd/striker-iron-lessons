Week 4 Day 2: Science - Conservation of Energy

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Yesterday we talked about stored (potential) and moving (kinetic) energy. Today we learn the rule of the universe: Energy cannot be created or destroyed, only changed! When you kick a ball, you are transferring energy, not making it out of nothing. Let's trace the energy!

Phase 1: The Law of Conservation

Energy cannot be created or destroyed.

It can only be transformed from one form to another, or transferred from one object to another.

Step 1: You have chemical energy from food.

Step 2: Your muscles turn that into kinetic energy to move your leg.

Phase 3: Where does the energy go?

When the ball stops, its kinetic energy isn't gone.

Friction turns the kinetic energy into heat energy (thermal energy) and sound energy!

  • I know friction creates heat.
  • I understand energy just changes type.

Phase 4: Mental Check

When you kick a ball, does the energy disappear when it hits the ground?
