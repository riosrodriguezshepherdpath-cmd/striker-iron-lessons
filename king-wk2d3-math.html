Week 2 Day 3: Math - Fractions

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Today we are adding and subtracting fractions. Think of this like sharing a soccer ball between teammates. If you have a common denominator, you just add the tops! Let's score big today!

Phase 1: Same Denominator Basics

Adding fractions with the same bottom number is very easy.

Just add the top numbers together and keep the bottom.

Example: 1/4 of the field plus 2/4 is 3/4.

  • I know to keep the denominator the same.
  • I can add numerators together.
  • I understand the soccer example.
‚òï QUICK BREAK: Stand up and stretch!

Phase 2: Subtracting Same Denominators

Subtracting works the same way as adding fractions.

Subtract the top number, and keep the bottom number.

Example: If you have 4/7 of your energy, and use 1/7, you have 3/7.

  • I know to subtract the numerators.
  • I know to keep the denominator.
  • I'm ready to move on.
‚òï HALFWAY THERE! Take a 1-minute break!

Phase 3: Different Denominators Intro

What if denominators are different? You cannot add them yet!

You must find a common denominator first, striker.

Think of making teams equal before the match begins.

  • I know I need a common denominator.
  • I understand why we need equal parts.
  • I am focused on the goal.

Phase 4: Practice Problems

Let's practice adding and subtracting fractions now.

Solve these problems carefully on your worksheet.

1) 1/6 + 3/6 = ?

2) 5/8 - 2/8 = ?

  • I solved the first problem.
  • I solved the second problem.
  • I reduced the fractions if needed.
‚òï ALMOST DONE! One more phase!

Phase 5: Physical Mission - Math Soccer

Place 6 cones in a row.

Dribble through all 6 cones.

After each dribble, shout out an answer to a fraction problem.

Examples: "What is 2/7 + 3/7?" or "What is 8/10 - 2/10?"
