Week 3 Day 3: Civics - The Executive Branch

Coach's Script: What's up striker! Yesterday we learned about Congress making the rules. Today, we are learning about the Executive Branch! This team makes sure the rules are followed. Think of them as the coaches and captains on the field ensuring players follow the rules! Let's learn how they work!

Phase 1: Enforcing the Rules

The Executive Branch enforces laws.

This branch is led by the President of the United States.

The President is like the Captain of the country!

If Congress makes a rule (Bill) the President does not like, they can say "NO".

This is called a Veto.

It's like the captain saying the play is too risky!
